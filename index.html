How does it work?

It's pretty simple. I use the Dumpling App to help you shop for your groceries. You can click this link to download it to your phone, and it will show you all of the local grocery stores and everything they have on their shelves. Choose me as your personal shopper, put together your order and then click OK, and I'll take care of the rest.

While I'm shopping, if anything you're looking for isn't available, I'll text or call you to see if you'd like a replacement. Then I'll bag it all up and bring it right to your door.

For anything else you need, just send me a message in the form down below. It will come directly to me, and I'll write you back as soon as I see your message to confirm I'm able to get what you need.

For grocery orders, Dumpling takes care of the payment processing. For anything else, you can pay by Venmo @letadamshop or Paypal letadamshop@gmail.com
