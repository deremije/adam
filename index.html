Welcome

George Donnelly specializes in US taxes and return preparation for clients with U.S. tax issues.  I focus on tax return preparation for U.S. and non U.S. citizens, tax planning advice, representation of taxpayers in front of the IRS in audit situations, and related services.

I am a boutique practice specializing in U.S. tax issues that arise for persons living outside the United States.  My clients consist primarily of U.S. citizens and long-term green card holders living in France.

The goal of my firm is to ensure that my client’s tax and business needs are fully met. I specialize in providing complete solutions to my clients with in-house services and through longstanding relationships with related service providers.

Services I Provide

Tax Preparation & Planning

Effective tax preparation and planning can help you to minimize your future tax liability. We can help you proactively manage both your personal and your business tax issues, including understanding how upcoming business opportunities impact your tax status and vice versa. Not all tax planning opportunities are readily apparent. By having us on your team, you are more likely to benefit from those opportunities. We understand how the latest federal, state and local tax legislation and other developments affect you and your business and we are constantly identifying new ways to reduce federal, state or local tax liabilities.

IRS Representation

An IRS audit can be an intimidating and complex process. If you or your business face an IRS audit, we can bring to bear years of experience in dealing with tax matters and IRS audit procedures to ensure that you are properly represented when dealing with the IRS and other tax authorities.

Meet George

George is a certified public accountant specializing in tax planning and preparation for US citizens residing abroad. As an expatriate himself, George fully understands the US and many foreign tax issues associated with living abroad. His international experience includes analyzing income tax treaty benefits, and assisting with transfer pricing documentation. George is also a Chartered Global Management Accountant and has a Master’s degree in Taxation from Golden Gate University.

With more than 25 years experience, George is often called upon to speak for professional organizations and has authored articles on various tax-related topics.
